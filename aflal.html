I'm Aflal, and These Are My Favorite Movies

Three unforgettable films that changed the way I see storytelling.

NO. 01

Interstellar (2014)

Interstellar is a science fiction masterpiece directed by Christopher Nolan. The film combines space exploration with emotional storytelling. The relationship between Cooper and Murph makes the journey memorable while the visuals and soundtrack create an unforgettable cinematic experience.

★★★★★

NO. 02

Inception (2010)

Inception explores dreams within dreams through a brilliant mix of action, psychology and mystery. Creative visual effects and an intelligent story keep every scene exciting from beginning to end.

★★★★★

NO. 03

The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King (2003)

This epic fantasy adventure delivers breathtaking battles, emotional moments and inspiring characters. Friendship, courage and sacrifice make this one of the greatest films ever made.

★★★★★
