I'm Thiviyashagar, and my Favourite Movies

Welcome to my movie blog. Here are my top three favourite movies.

Avengers: Endgame

Avengers: Endgame is a superhero movie released in 2019. The Avengers travel through time to collect the Infinity Stones and defeat Thanos. It is one of the highest-grossing movies ever made.

Titanic

Titanic is a romantic drama directed by James Cameron. The movie tells the unforgettable love story of Jack and Rose during the tragic sinking of the RMS Titanic.

Spider-Man: No Way Home

Spider-Man: No Way Home follows Peter Parker after his identity is revealed to the world. He seeks help from Doctor Strange, leading to an exciting adventure across the multiverse.
