Thushann's Movie Showcase

A curated collection of my all-time favorite cinematic experiences.

3 Idiots

Comedy / Drama

Two friends embark on a quest to find their long-lost companion from engineering college. Along the way, they revisit their campus days and recall the friend who inspired them to think differently, even as the world labeled them "idiots."

The Dark Knight

Action / Crime / Drama

When the menace known as the Joker emerges from his mysterious past, he wreaks havoc and chaos on the people of Gotham. Batman must accept one of the greatest psychological and physical tests of his ability to fight injustice.

Interstellar

Sci-Fi / Adventure

As Earth's future becomes increasingly uninhabitable, a team of explorers travels through a newly discovered wormhole in space. They face unimaginable challenges and heartbreaking choices in an attempt to ensure humanity's survival.
